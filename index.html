About Us

With the homeless population in the Tenderloin area of San Francisco being 51% of the total homeless population in the city, HopeSF guarantees that their health will be taken care of so they have one less thing to worry about. Our company believes that everyone has a right to standard living and maintaining adequate health despite the circumstances. HopeSF will provide reliable tools for communication through Kiosks and the means of health care through accessible pop-up trucks, all supported through partnerships with different companies, UCSF health care providers, and UCSF student interns.
